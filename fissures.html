Warframe Void Fissures Guide

Void Fissures are special missions which work very similarly to regular missions. Before starting a void fissure, you can pick a void relic to open, and in addition to the mission objective, you must collect 10 reactant* to open that void relic.

Reactant drop from corrupted enemies. During a void fissure, corrupted enemies will occasionally spawn in, and when that happens, normal enemies around that spot get corrupted as well. This typically happens shortly after at least half of the tenno squad gets close to a new group of enemies.

The fastest void fissure missions Void fissures take some time to complete, obviously. Since the reward for completing a void fissure of different missions is the same, if we want the largest number of rewards, it makes sense to go for the ones that take the least amount of time.

Here is a list of the fastest void fissure missions:

Top Tier:
Lith: Mariana, Earth.       Meso: Pallas, Ceres.       Neo: Armaros, Europa.       Axi: Saxis, Eris.

Second Best:
Lith: E Prime, Earth.       Meso: Roche, Phobos.       Neo: Baal, Europa.       Axi: Isos, Eris.

All Void missions award a relic upon completion, but they might be somewhat slower because of the void tileset.

The following missions drop Lith relics on completion:
Hepit, Void – neo/axi capture.       Teshub, Void – neo/axi exterminate.

The following missions drop Meso/Neo relics on completion:
Ukko, Void – neo/axi capture.       Oxomoco, Void – neo/axi exterminate.

Strategy As you may have noticed, the quickest void fissures in the list above are mostly exterminate missions, and a few of them are captures. The thing they all have in common is that all you have to do to complete them is move forward, kill enemies and collect reactant, and maybe capture one target.

This is why the loadouts which allow you to complete these missions the fastest, always include a Warframe that can move fast, and a way to kill many enemies fast, usually with the Warframe's abilities, but possibly with other equipment.

Warframe.school has a separate page about the best Warframe builds for void fissures.

These are the things which you should never forget:

Never go into a void fissure without a void relic.

Always try your best to not fall behind the other players, in terms of your position on the map. even using a starter Warframe, it is possible to keep up with almost anyone if your parkour skills are good.

Here are some things which improve QOL when doing void fissures:

Use a companion with the (MAXED!) mod [Vacuum] for robotic companions or [Fetch] if you have an animal.

Go for the fastest void fissure missions that were mentioned before, preferably those that you can complete relatively fast even without help.

Good luck!

*The plural form of Warframe's "reactant" is "reactant".
