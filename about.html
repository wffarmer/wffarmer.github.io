About Warframe.school

Warframe.school provides guides and videos about Warframe. Warframe.school has no official affiliation with Digital Extremes or Warframe.

Warframe.school was created by one person, because that person wanted to get some experience at running a website and running a YouTube channel, while also trying to help bring people to a deep understanding of the fundamentals of Warframe.

www.warframe.school became public on June 14, 2024, and since then, content kept being added to the site and the YouTube channel.

Thank you for studying with us!
